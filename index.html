How We Craft
Excellence

01

Select & Grade the Hide

Each side is hand-inspected by our master tanner, graded for consistency, thickness, and fiber tightness before entering inventory.

02

Traditional Tannage

Vegetable tanning uses bark, oak gall, and sumac — a 6-week process that creates unparalleled firmness and future patina.

03

Measure, Cut & Ship

Hides are measured precisely, wrapped in kraft paper, and shipped within 48 hours in climate-controlled packaging to preserve quality.
